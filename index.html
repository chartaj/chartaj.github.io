CHARTAJ and CHARTUIE-8K:

Rich AI Feedback for Chart Generation

Anonymous Authors

Abstract

The scarcity of cheap, quality chart ⟨instruction, data, code, image⟩quadruplets continues to impede advancements in LLM-generated charts. In response, we introduce two novel contributions: ChartUIE-8K, a large-scale, user-centric evaluation dataset, and ChartAJ, the first effective reference-free auto-judge tailored towards chart generation feedback. ChartUIE-8K offers diverse and realistic queries, with over 8,000 queries, a significant increase in chart types, topics, and underlying data sets, compared to existing works. ChartAJ provides rich AI feedback, empowering models to improve through context-tuning and test-time scaling without the need for costly training data sets. Extensive double-blind studies confirm that ChartAJ delivers state-of-the-art performance in automatic evaluation, surpassing existing methods in feedback efficacy per token cost. Both contributions are open-source at chartaj.github.io, offering a substantial resource for future advancements in large language models and chart generation.
